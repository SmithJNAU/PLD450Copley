    • Virtual Reality Therapy

    Virtual Reality Therapy, is a new, unique and innovative approach to traditional therapy. Traditional therapy involves sitting face to face, and communicating with one another. Teletherapy has started to become more popular, as well as mobile applications in order to schedule appointments, and even communicate with your therapist. Virtual Reality Exposure Therapy, or VRET for short is an underutilized type of therapy that is able to treat many different mental health issues such as anxiety, social anxiety, depression, PTSD, as well as certain fears such as spiders, or public speaking.

    Find a Therapist or VRET Provider

    VRET is a well tested and well studied type of therapy that not only provides great results, but it can quickly provide meaningful change in your life. If you are interested in finding a local therapist, or a nationwide provider of VRET, click the "Find a Therapist Link above".
